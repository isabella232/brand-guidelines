Basic Logo Usage

Light logos are for dark backgrounds and vice versa
Use the Rancher Labs logos when referencing the company
Use the Rancher logos when referencing the product
Always use a cow with a registration mark
Don't use the full color logo on dark backgrounds
...or on a busy
background
Don't change the color of the logo
Don't obsruct the logo
